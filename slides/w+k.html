Microcontrollers

  • It's just a small computer
  • You can program it: tell it what to do
  • Those pins connect it to the world
  • INPUTS en OUTPUTS
  • OUTPUT: "Turn on the light!"
  • INPUT: "Is the light on?

The Arduino

  • It's just a microcontroller
  • Soldered on a board, to make it easy
  • You program it on the computer, then upload it to the arduino
